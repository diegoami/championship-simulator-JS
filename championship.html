This page has no practical use. It simulates a round-robin tournament among teams in several leagues, whose relative strength was taken from this page and this page in October 2012. It was made as en exercise in Javascript. Random rounds are played automatically. Use the buttons to pause and unpause, to go faster or slower through the rounds. Pick a league in the combobox and click on the restart button to restart the championship. Scroll down for a complete championship result history.
